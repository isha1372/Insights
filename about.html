About Gazva Insights

What we publish, who writes for us, and the standard we hold ourselves to.

Gazva Insight is the official publication platform of the Gazva Students' Union of Fathima Zahra Islamic Women's College, Chemmad. Dedicated to knowledge, faith, and creativity, it features articles on the Quran, Fiqh, Islamic history, contemporary issues, and student reflections, alongside stories, poetry, and other creative works.

What we publish

Our Quran section explores the structure, language, and meaning of the Quran. Fiqh covers Islamic rulings and the reasoning behind them, ensuring that the "why" is never left out. Thareekh brings Islamic history to life through its people, pivotal moments, and enduring lessons rather than dates alone. General is where we publish reflections on a wide range of topics, from faith and society to contemporary issues. Kurippathukal is our creative corner for short stories, poetry, personal reflections, and other literary works.

Our editorial standard

Every article is reviewed before publishing, with sources noted where relevant. When scholars differ on a question, we try to represent that difference honestly rather than flattening it into a single answer.
